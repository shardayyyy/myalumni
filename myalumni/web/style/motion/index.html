Welcome

Hello and welcome to Motion, a free template by Six Shooter Media. For this template I have gone in a completely different direction and created something quite specific in its design ideals. Motion is designed for someone wishing to display a fairly small amount of information, elegantly in a single page whilst still be able to wow the user. The accordion effect used in this template is based on this fabulous piece of work which you should refer to for any help/discussion regarding the Javascript used within this template.

The design is simple, but should be elegant and innovative enough to engage the user and make them want to read more about your company/product. Orange was the colour chosen for the basis of the colour scheme but you could of course easily change that.
